Interesting facts about chameleon

  1. The eyes of the chameleon provide 360 degree vision due to unique eye anatomy and an ability to transition between monocular and binocular vision.
  2. They mainly change color in order to communicate or regulate body temperature.
  3. According to the Integrated Taxonomic Information System (ITIS), there are 171 species of chameleons.
